ABOUT

I am a Software Engineer from Indonesia based in Singapore.

I graduated from National University of Singapore with a degree in Computer Engineering in May 2020. I did my dissertation on Natural Language Processing for academic application.

I enjoy exploring various topics in computer science. During my university years, I took wide ranging modules from Design and Analysis of Algorithm, to Computer Security to Database Implementation to Machine Learning to Big Data Systems among others.

Currently, I am a backend engineer with a moderate grasp on modern front-end techniques. I am striving to be better at front-end development in order to create more user-fiendly softwares. Also, I used to be a native iOS developer before making the switch.

When I am not busy coding, you can find me playing sports (I am always up for some badminton or table tennis games), reading, or polishing my Chinese and Korean.
