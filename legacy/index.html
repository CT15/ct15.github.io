About Me

My name's Calvin. I am a Final Year Computer Engineering undergraduate at the National University of Singapore. I'm always excited to meet like-minded individuals who are also passionate about software development. Currently, I'm most interested in mobile development and Natural Language Processing.


University education has played a huge role in shaping who I am today. I started as an Electrical Engineering student who knew nothing about programming. However, after taking my compulsory Programming Methodology course, I knew that I was in the wrong major. I switched my major in the following semester and relentlessly exposed myself to new technologies and knowledge. University education has turned me from a person who believed that he wasn't a tech person to a tech enthusiast and a Hackathon addict!


When I'm not coding, you can find me playing table tennis, playing badminton, watching anime, reading, or learning foreign languages.
